Miller Creek Camp


Welcome to your outdoor adventure!

Miller Creek Kid's Camp is a 3 to 5 day wilderness retreat for kids to experience the outdoors, wildlife, and learn important survival skills! A brief description of activities includes river rafting, hiking, camping, cooking, hunting, as well as much, much, more! Your youngin's will enjoy experiencing their backyard in this whole new way!


3 vs 5 day options

There are two camp options a 5-day retreat and a 3-day retreat. The 5-day retreat includes one day for rafting, two days for camping, one day talking to a survival expert, and the last day cleaning camp up and showing family members and friends all that they have learned in an afternoon picnic! The 3-day option allows one day for rafting and swimming, one day for hiking and hunting(if allowed), and the last day for an activity of their choice.

Activity Options List:

  • Swimming at Bunkhouse
  • Hike up Mount Sentinal
  • Hike back up in Miller Creek
  • Lolo Hot Springs (or other Hot Springs)
  • Community Service Project in one of our local parks
